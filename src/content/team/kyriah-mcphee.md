Kyriah leads MESCo's engineering and quality systems portfolio, transforming project intent into clear, auditable frameworks that hold up under scrutiny. Her focus is on QA/QC integration, mobilisation planning, and ISO-aligned documentation that make compliance both practical and defensible.

Her approach balances technical rigour with site-based reality, producing systems that perform equally well in audits and in the field.

## Key Strengths

- **QA/QC Framework Design:** Development of structured, audit-ready systems aligned to ISO 9001 and major client specifications.
- **Mobilisation Planning:** Establishing project documentation, staging tools, and controlled registers that enable seamless project start-up.
- **Engineering Administration:** Coordination of ITPs, lot registers, and verification workflows ensuring traceable, compliant close-out.
- **System Integration:** Translating complex project and client requirements into simple, defensible documentation frameworks.
- **ISO Alignment:** Practical implementation of ISO 9001, 14001 and 45001 principles into everyday project delivery.
