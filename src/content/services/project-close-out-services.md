## Overview

Close-out is where loose ends show. Without clear systems, documentation,
defects, and certifications can drag on long after construction is finished.

MESCo designs close-out frameworks that help you finish projects cleanly, with
documentation and records ready for clients and regulators.

## What’s included

- Defect and action tracking registers
- Certification and documentation checklists
- Handover pack structures and templates
- Support to reconcile outstanding items and records

## How it works

1. Define close-out requirements with your team and client.
2. Build the registers, trackers, and checklists needed to get there.
3. Support your team as they gather and reconcile evidence and documentation.

## Who it’s for

This service is ideal for:

- Contractors wanting predictable, low-stress close-outs
- Clients who need confidence that records and certifications are complete
