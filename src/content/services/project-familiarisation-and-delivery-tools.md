## Overview

Even on well-documented projects, engineers and supervisors often start with a
pile of drawings, specifications, and contracts but no clear way to translate
them into day-to-day delivery.

MESCo builds simple, structured tools that help people understand the job fast
and deliver it with confidence.

## The problem

Without the right delivery tools:

- Critical requirements stay buried in documents.
- Hold points and inspections are easy to miss.
- New team members take longer to become productive.

## What’s included

Typical support includes:

- Project familiarisation packs and key-scope summaries
- Mapping of scope to hold points, ITPs, and inspection requirements
- Delivery trackers for quality, documentation, and close-out
- Tools that link drawings, specifications, and field tasks

## How it works

1. Review project documentation and contract requirements.
2. Identify critical scope, interfaces, and risk points.
3. Build simple tools and trackers aligned to how your team works.
4. Walk through the tools with your site team and adjust as needed.

## Deliverables

You receive practical, editable tools such as:

- Familiarisation briefs and checklists
- Scope and hold-point mapping sheets
- Delivery trackers for inspections, documentation, and close-out

## Who it’s for

This service is ideal for:

- Engineers stepping into complex or fast-moving projects
- Site teams who need clearer visibility over what must be delivered and when
