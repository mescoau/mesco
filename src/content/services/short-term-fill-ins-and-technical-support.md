## Overview

Projects do not always line up neatly with available people. When key staff are
on leave, overloaded, or pulled into other work, important systems can stall.

MESCo provides short-term engineering and systems support so your projects stay
on track and audit-ready.

## What’s included

- Short-term fill-ins for engineering or systems roles
- Support for documentation, registers, and audit preparation
- Stabilisation of existing systems and identification of improvements

## How it works

1. Agree on the scope and duration of support.
2. Integrate MESCo into your existing team and systems.
3. Focus on high-impact tasks that reduce risk and backlog.

## Who it’s for

This service is ideal for:

- Projects experiencing resourcing gaps or workload spikes
- Teams needing temporary help to get systems back under control
