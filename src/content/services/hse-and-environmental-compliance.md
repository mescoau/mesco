## Overview

Site teams juggle production, people, and paperwork every day. Without clear,
manageable HSE and environmental systems, important controls can slip and audit
exposure increases.

MESCo develops and maintains frameworks that keep your projects compliant and
your teams focused on safe, efficient delivery.

## The problem

When HSE and environmental systems are not well-structured:

- SWMSs and plans are scattered and inconsistent.
- Environmental reporting is reactive instead of planned.
- Audit findings are harder to close and keep closed.

## What’s included

- WHS Management Plans (WHSMPs)
- Safe Work Method Statements (SWMSs)
- Environmental plans and reporting structures
- Toolbox talk packs and communication templates
- Site audit tools and close-out documentation

## How it works

1. Review project scope, risk profile, and legislative requirements.
2. Create or refine HSE and environmental plans and documentation packs.
3. Support ongoing site audits, updates, and close-out reporting.

## Deliverables

You receive:

- Editable plan templates and SWMS examples
- Toolbox and briefing packs
- Audit registers and close-out documentation records

## Who it’s for

This service is ideal for:

- Contractors needing complete, audit-ready HSE and environmental systems
- Clients who require consistent safety and environmental performance across projects
