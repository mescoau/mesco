## Overview

ISO frameworks set clear expectations for how work is planned, delivered, and
recorded. The challenge is turning those expectations into simple systems that
teams can follow in the real world.

MESCo helps you design project systems that align with ISO requirements and
support you through audits and ongoing compliance.

## What’s included

- Review of current systems against ISO requirements
- Documentation and process mapping
- Guidance on roles, responsibilities, and records
- Support to prepare evidence packs for audits

## How it works

1. Assess where your current systems align with ISO and where gaps exist.
2. Design or refine processes and documentation to close those gaps.
3. Support your team to prepare for and navigate audits.

## Who it’s for

This service is ideal for:

- Contractors and clients operating under ISO frameworks
- Projects needing stronger alignment between site practice and ISO requirements
