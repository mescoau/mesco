## Overview

Engineering teams are often responsible for both technical delivery and the
administration that proves it. Without clear systems, important information
quickly becomes hard to find and harder to trust.

MESCo designs and maintains the systems that sit behind engineering and project
administration so work is traceable, auditable, and easier to manage.

## What’s included

- Engineering and document control registers
- Workflows for RFIs, design changes, and approvals
- Standardised templates and naming conventions
- Guidance on how the systems should be used in the field

## How it works

1. Review current engineering and admin practices.
2. Map out required registers, workflows, and approvals.
3. Build or refine the systems to match your project and team.
4. Support rollout and ongoing adjustments as the project evolves.

## Who it’s for

This service is ideal for:

- Projects where engineering and administration are handled by the same team
- Contractors needing better structure behind RFIs, approvals, and documentation
