## Overview

Projects often begin before the systems and paperwork are ready. Site teams rush
to assemble registers, plans, and checklists while work is already underway.
That creates stress, delays, and audit risk.

MESCo steps in before the first shovel hits the ground. We design and assemble
the project start-up systems so your team begins with clear, compliant,
audit-ready documentation.

## The problem

Without proper start-up systems:

- Registers are built reactively and often miss key items.
- Compliance documents are scattered across inboxes and personal folders.
- Mobilisation takes longer, and audits are harder to pass.

## What’s included

Typical start-up support includes:

- WHS Management Plans (WHSMPs)
- Risk registers
- Project management plans
- Mobilisation checklists
- Document and folder structures for site, engineering, and management teams

## How it works

1. Review design deliverables, contract scope, and client requirements.
2. Build project-specific templates, plans, and registers.
3. Prepare mobilisation documentation and checklists.
4. Deliver a structured start-up pack to the site team for launch.

## Deliverables

You receive ready-to-use templates and registers, such as:

- Editable WHSMPs and project management plans
- Risk registers aligned to your scope and risk profile
- Mobilisation checklists and folder structures

## Who it’s for

This service is ideal for:

- Contractors and subcontractors mobilising new projects or packages
- EPCM clients who need consistent, audit-ready project systems
